About Luxova

A Legacy of
Illumination

Luxova is a premium Middle East lighting brand with over 14 years of expertise in architectural, commercial, and outdoor lighting solutions. Founded in Dubai, we operate state-of-the-art R&D facilities and dedicated testing laboratories to engineer luminaires that meet the most demanding architectural specifications.

ISO 9001 & ISO 14001 certified manufacturing processes ensuring consistent quality across all products.
In-house photometric testing laboratory with LM-79/LM-80 certification capabilities.
12+ international design patents protecting our proprietary luminaire technologies.
Dedicated lighting design team providing DIALux simulations and engineering support.
500+ completed projects across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman.
